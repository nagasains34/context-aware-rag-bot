# Company FAQ – NovaCart Customer Support

## 1. Refund Policy

At NovaCart, customer satisfaction is our highest priority. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund within 30 days from the date your order was delivered. To qualify for a refund, the product must be returned in its original condition, unused, and with all original packaging and accessories.

Refund requests can be submitted through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer support team. Once we receive the returned product, our quality inspection team will verify its condition. If the return is approved, the refund will be processed within 5 to 7 business days. The refunded amount will be credited to the original payment method used during the purchase.

Some products are not eligible for refunds. These include digital products that have already been downloaded, gift cards, customized items, and products marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase. Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the return is due to an error made by NovaCart or if the product arrived damaged.

If your product arrives damaged or defective, please report the issue within 48 hours of delivery. Our team will arrange a replacement or a full refund after verification.

---

## 2. Delivery Timelines

NovaCart works with multiple logistics partners to ensure timely delivery of orders. Standard delivery usually takes between 3 and 7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities generally receive deliveries faster, while remote locations may require additional delivery time.

Express delivery is available for selected products and locations. Orders placed before 2:00 PM on business days are usually processed on the same day. Orders placed after business hours, on weekends, or on public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

Customers receive a tracking number once the order has been shipped. The tracking link can be used to monitor the delivery status in real time. Delivery timelines may occasionally be affected due to weather conditions, natural disasters, transportation issues, or other unforeseen circumstances.

If your order has not arrived within the estimated delivery period, please contact customer support with your order number so that we can investigate the delay.

---

## 3. Membership Benefits

NovaCart offers three membership tiers: Silver, Gold, and Platinum.

Silver members receive access to exclusive promotional offers, order tracking, email support, and seasonal discounts.

Gold members enjoy all Silver benefits along with priority order processing, faster refunds, early access to special sales, and free standard shipping on eligible orders.

Platinum members receive the highest level of service. In addition to all Gold benefits, they receive premium customer support, dedicated account assistance, priority issue resolution, exclusive product launches, free express shipping on eligible orders, and invitations to special promotional events.

Membership status is automatically linked to your registered account. The chatbot and customer support team may use your membership tier to provide personalized assistance and explain benefits available to your account.

---

## 4. Account Cancellation

Customers may cancel their NovaCart account at any time by submitting an account deletion request through the account settings page or by contacting customer support.

Before your account is permanently deleted, all pending orders must be completed or cancelled. Any refunds that are currently being processed will continue even after the account deletion request has been submitted.

Once the account has been deleted, personal information associated with the account will be removed according to our privacy policy, except where retention is required by applicable laws or financial regulations.

Please note that deleted accounts cannot be recovered. Customers wishing to use NovaCart again must register a new account.

Membership benefits associated with the deleted account will also be permanently removed and cannot be transferred to another account.

---

## 5. Support Contact Hours

NovaCart customer support is available Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM local time.

Customers can reach us through live chat, email, or our customer support phone line during business hours. Email requests received outside business hours will be answered on the next working day.

Our AI-powered customer support assistant is available 24 hours a day to answer common questions related to orders, refunds, delivery status, membership benefits, and account management.

For urgent delivery issues, customers are encouraged to contact live support during operating hours for faster assistance.

---

## 6. Premium Customer Support Rules

Premium customer support is available only for Platinum members. Platinum customers receive the highest priority when contacting customer support through chat, email, or phone.

Premium support includes dedicated support representatives, priority ticket handling, reduced waiting times, and faster issue resolution whenever possible. Platinum members also receive assistance with complex order issues, refund escalations, and account-related concerns.

Gold members receive priority processing compared to Silver members but do not receive dedicated premium support representatives.

Silver members receive standard customer support services during normal business hours.

If a customer asks for premium support but does not have Platinum membership, the chatbot should politely explain that premium customer support is available only for Platinum members. However, all customers will continue to receive regular customer support according to their membership level.

For technical issues affecting all users, NovaCart provides equal support to every customer regardless of membership tier until the issue is resolved.
